It's a pretty simple modal window at this point, but the beauty of in it is that it is responsive as well. Try resizing your browser and watch what happens. When you let go of your browser window, the modal will recenter itself. All of the styling can be changed as well. If you don't like the close button, it can be easily removed. Simply go to the bottom of the openModal function and follow the instructions.

To close the modal you can either click the close button, click anywhere on the overlay (outside of the modal window), or hit the ESC key on your keyboard.
